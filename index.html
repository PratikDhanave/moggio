mog is a multi-source music player

Usage

To use mog, start the application, then open your web browser to http://localhost:6601.

Then add a protocol (source). Your music files will be added to the Music page shortly after.

Sources

mog can play from various sources:

  • Google Music
  • Your computer
  • Soundcloud
  • Dropbox
  • Google Drive
  • Shoutcast Streaming
  • Bandcamp

mog can play the following file formats:

  • MP3
  • NSF (Nintendo Sound Format)
  • WAV
    • flac
